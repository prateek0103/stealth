Stealth is Vellore's only gaming lounge, giving a great experience to casual and hardcore gamers alike. A social platform for gamers, in association with VIT's Technology and Gaming Club, gives rising eSports players a chance to try and become the best.

Over 20 PC, three Xbox One and three PlayStation 4 consoles fill the industrial, techy space to immerse you into the the latest and greatest games from League of Legends and Call of Duty to WWE and Street Fighter.

Games aren’t the only thing Stealth has to offer. To keep the gamers fueled for their skirmishes, beverages and snacks are also available.
