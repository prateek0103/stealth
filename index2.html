Ignite is Chicago's premier gaming lounge, offering an innovative entertainment option since 2002. Enter a unique social environment that redefines the way you play video games with your friends. Over 90 PC, Xbox, PlayStation and Wii consoles fill the industrial, techy space to immerse you into the the latest and greatest games from League of Legends and Call of Duty to Madden and Street Fighter. Ignite Gaming Lounge gives your classic arcade a next-gen overhaul.

Games aren’t the only thing Ignite serves up. A snack bar designed to keep you fueled delivers tasty made to order concessions well into the evening. Tournaments and events, high-performance gear, and a lineup of friendly and knowledgeable staff complete a one of a kind gaming experience. More »
